About Me

Currently I am a Ph.D Candidate in the Department of Computer Science and Technology at Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Lifeng Sun . I received my bachelor degree from Department of Computer Science and Technology at Harbin Engineering University in 2021. My research work focuses on the multimedia network streaming, including adaptive video streaming and congestion control.
